American Heart Association Certified Courses

The AHA’s BLS course trains participants to promptly recognize several life-threatening emergencies, give high-quality chest compressions, deliver appropriate ventilations and provide early use of an AED.

The American Heart Association’s ACLS (Advanced Cardiovascular Life Support ) Course continues on the foundation of lifesaving BLS highlighting the importance of continuous, high-quality CPR.

For healthcare providers who respond to emergencies in infants and children and for personnel in emergency response, emergency medicine, intensive care and critical care unit.
